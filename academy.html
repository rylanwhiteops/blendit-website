The Curriculum

WHAT YOU
ACTUALLY LEARN

No filler. No theory-only sessions. Every class puts tools in your hands and knowledge in your head. By week 8 you're not a student. You're a barber.

Taught by Sajjad and Hayzo, the same barbers who built Blendit from zero to Stoney Creek's top shop before they turned 21.

BLURRY FADES & SKIN FADES

The bread and butter. Master the fade that built Blendit's entire reputation. From mid-fades to bald fades with zero lines.

RAZOR WORK & LINE-UPS

Sharp lines separate good barbers from great ones. Learn edge-up techniques and razor blade handling that clients screenshot and send to friends.

BEARD SCULPTING & DESIGN

Shape, style, and structure. Beard work that completes the look and doubles your service revenue per client.

THE BUSINESS OF BARBERING

Clientele growth, pricing, Instagram strategy, and building a book from zero. The full playbook that Sajjad and Hayzo actually used.

HAIR TEXTURE & ADVANCED CUTS

Curly, wavy, thick, fine. Handle every hair type with confidence. Afros, tapers, textured cuts, and everything in between.

Semester 2 Schedule

8 WEEKS.
ONE LEVEL UP.

W1

FOUNDATIONS

Tools, station setup, hygiene, posture, and the mindset that separates professionals from amateurs.

W2

CLIPPER CONTROL

Technique, pressure, angles. Build the muscle memory that makes fades effortless.

W3

MID FADES & TAPERS

Core fade techniques on real models. Clean blends, clean transitions.

W4

SKIN FADES & BALD FADES

The advanced version. Zero guard work, shadow fades, and the hardest transitions in the game.

W5

LINE-UPS, RAZOR WORK & DESIGNS

Edges that clients post on their Instagram. Razors, straight-line tools, and creative add-ons.

W6

BEARD SCULPTING

Full beard styling, trim techniques, shape-ups, and moisturize-and-style finishes.

W7

THE BUSINESS PLAYBOOK

Build your book, set your prices, grow your Instagram, and retain clients. The Blendit business model, open book.

W8

FINAL SHOWCASE

Cut on real clients. Get your certificate. Launch your career. This is the day you've been building toward.
